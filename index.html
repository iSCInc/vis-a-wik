see a related short paper

Visualize and compare Wikipedia articles, and their
links,in two different language editions, as a network
diagram. Search for Wikipedia articles, click on each
article that you like to add articles to the drawing list
— possibly search for, and add more articles —
then click "Draw". Drag nodes to change the
configuration of the network diagram. Changing the
language options will clear the drawing list.
